Channels

Channels are content collections created by vendors. After the channel created by the vendor, it should be approved by vendors to publish.

Go to channels list and click on the “Edit” icon and change channel status to “Active”.

You see “Verification Status” field, What does it mean?

Vendors can promote their channels to official or verified channels. This feature is similar to the “Blue Badge” on Instagram. Vendors can send their verification documents, and you can review them and change channel verification status to “Verified”.

You can see channel verification requests under the “Verification requests” menu.
